--- layout: archive title: "Milk & Honey" permalink: /milkandhoneymamas/ author_profile: true --- ## Milk & Honey Mamas Milk & Honey Mamas is a website I run meeting children where they are with theological ideas and practices. I believe that children are intelligent, creative, and insightful humans who do not need our theological crumbs. They need the full meal, at the big table, laden with milk and honey. I run this website with two colleagues.
